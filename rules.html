Rules and Regulations

All students at RAVOS should abide by the following rules:


  • Each learner shall be responsible for his/her conduct from time to time
  • RAVOS shall not tolerate intentional false reporting of incidents
  • Learners shall be expected to uphold and abide by certain standards of conduct
  • Academic misconducts such as plagiarism, copying, violating course rules shall not be tolerated
  • Acts of dishonesty like forgery, submitting flase infomation to the school or initiating a false warning of fire

Preamble

RAVOS Technical College has a high standard of conduct for members of its college community including students. Consistent with the educational mission of the College, RAVOS students are expected to perform all work honestly, maintain prescribed academic standards, pay all debts to the College and respect the property and right of others.


Guiding Principles

  1. To ensure the maintenance of an environment conducive to the attainment of the educational mission of the RAVOS College, the Principal is authorized to establish such rules and regulations as are deemed appropriate and necessary for the orderly operation of the College.

  2. The student conduct process at RAVOS is not intended to punish students. Rather, it exists to protect the interests of the College community and to challenge those whose behavior is not in accordance with our policies. Sanctions are intended to challenge students’ moral and ethical decision-making and to help them bring their behavior into accord with our community expectations.

  3. When a student is unable to conform his/her behavior to community expectation, the student conduct process may determine that he/she should no longer share in the privilege of participating in the College community.

  4. Students should be aware that the student conduct process is different from criminal and civil court proceedings. Procedures and rights in student conduct proceedings are conducted with fairness to all, but do not include the same protection of due process afforded by the courts.

  5. No student will be found in violation of RAVOS Student Code of Conduct policy without information showing by prevalence of the evidence that a policy violation occurred. In RAVOS Student Code of Conduct’s sole discretion, sanctions will be proportionate to the severity of the violation.


About Us

RAVOS Technical College is a Trust located in Kanye, established in 1986 and registered with the Ministry of Education and Skills Development as a private vocational school in January 1990.
At RAVOS Tech we are a value driven community. Our values ensure that our day to day lives are manageable and make us produce better results.
