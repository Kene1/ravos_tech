General Admissions Information

Our target population is the junior and senior school leavers. Anyone who has their junior or senior certificate is legible to apply. Applicants need to submit their certified copies of certificates and ID documents.


Registration fee for all courses is a non-refundable P100 and it is payable before the commencement of the course.

Core Admissions Principles


The Admissions and Enrolment Policy applies to prospective students, staff and management.


Our Admissions Principles are as follows:


  • The Academic Committee ensures that all information necessary for applicants to accept an offer is made available
  • Students' enrolment and registration records shall be kept in the Records Management Unit and made available when required by an authorised person.
  • Readmission after absence or exclusion shall be consistent with approved minimum entry requirements and selection criteria.
  • International students holding study permits shall be required to complete their courses within the original expected duration.
  • Publication of minimum entry requiremnts and enrolment information and deadlines shall be the responsibility of the Academic Commitee. This shall be published on the College prospectus or website
  • The HOD shall be responsible for the enrolment process which must be consistent with the selection criteria.

Admission Criteria


The College, when setting the minimum entry requirements, shall be guided by the set principles.


Some of the principles are as follows:


  • Clear and consistent admission requirements that are compliant with BQA framework
  • Evidence based admissions requirements
  • Eliminating unfair or unnecessary barriers for student access to RAVOS courses
  • Enabling flexible qualification pathway
  • Facilitating recognition or credit for entry into RAVOS.

The criteria for minimum entry requiremnts shall be inclusive and are set out as follows:


  • Academic Merit
  • Equivalency
  • Prior learning
  • Other experiences and attainments
  • Enlish language proficiency


Inclusive Environment


At RAVOS, we are a diverse College which accepts able bodied and disabled persons with zero tolerance for discrimination.


  • The College Academic Committee shall provide all information necessary for applicants to be admitted to the College.
  • The College shall not discriminate any applicant because of disability, racial/national, religious or disadvantaged groups as stated in RAVOS Equal Opportunities Policy Document attached.
  • The College shall promote equal educational opportunities to all students and student applicants for admission and enrolment.


About Us

RAVOS Technical College is a Trust located in Kanye, established in 1986 and registered with the Ministry of Education and Skills Development as a private vocational school in January 1990.
At RAVOS Tech we are a value driven community. Our values ensure that our day to day lives are manageable and make us produce better results.
