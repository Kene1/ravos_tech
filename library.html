Library Services


The following services are offered in our library:


  • Book lending

    • We offer this service to all Ravos students and Staff.The books are available on a short term basis.


  • Inter Library Loaning

    • The school has made an arrangement with University of Botswana Library to source out some learning material.


  • References Services

    • Under this service the books are not allowed to leave the library. They can only be used inside the library.


Library Rules

The library books are borrowed only for two weeks but students are allowed to extend the period if they feel they still need to use the material.


Overdue Charges


The following charges will be enforced if rules are broken


  • General Collection Books P5/day
  • Reserved Material P5/day
  • Unreturned Books P50/book

About Us

RAVOS Technical College is a Trust located in Kanye, established in 1986 and registered with the Ministry of Education and Skills Development as a private vocational school in January 1990.
At RAVOS Tech we are a value driven community. Our values ensure that our day to day lives are manageable and make us produce better results.
