Accomodation and Meals

The College has boarding and catering facilities for students. It also has a multi-purpose hall, a football field, library and Wi-Fi service. For training and production the college has poultry houses, abattoir, dress making room, hairdressing training room which is fully furnished with equipment, animals (cattle) and horticulture plots. The College boasts of having houses for training and support staff which are fully serviced with water and electric power. Our learning classrooms are in good condition to make sure our learners enjoy their studies.



Life at RAVOS College is full of pleasures and amusements. Food and lodgings are provided for students who are taking short courses. Accomodation is available for all the students but long-term students will have to self-cater.

College life at RAVOS encourages interaction and social development between students. Students can start social clubs that help them deal with everyday issues as well as academic issues.


Graduation Profile


The College holds inaugural Graduation ceremony in the college premises annually in September under different themes. In 2019 the theme was “Technical vocational education key to sustainable development”. Dignifying this event was the Assistant Minister of Tertiary Education, Science and Technology Honourable Moiseraele Goya.

Delivering the keynote address, Honourable Goya applauded RAVOS Technical College citing that its mandate to provide technical vocational education is vital as education is key to sustainable development. He added that one day, diamonds would have depleted, and as thus providing education is enhancing human capital who shall use the skills for job creation.

About Us

RAVOS Technical College is a Trust located in Kanye, established in 1986 and registered with the Ministry of Education and Skills Development as a private vocational school in January 1990.
At RAVOS Tech we are a value driven community. Our values ensure that our day to day lives are manageable and make us produce better results.
