Who We Are


History


RAVOS Technical College is an academic institution established by a Deed of Trust in 1986, it is located in Kanye and registered with the Ministry of Education and Skills Development as a private vocational school in January 1990. The concerned parties to the establishment of Ravos included Lutheran Churches, Minister of Education and Skills Development, Member of Parliament for Kanye, Kanye Local Authorities and Department of Secondary Education. Ravos is currently registered and accredited by BQA to offer vocational training.
