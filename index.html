Why study at Ravos?

At Ravos we offer a variety of certificate and diploma courses. We are a diverse college which accepts students from all over.


We have renowned experience and have established sound working relations and Social and Community Development departments countrywide. We offer practical programs to poverty eradication beneficiaries sponsored by various councils. We also give an opportunity to disadvantaged children to be equipped for self employment. We are continuing to equip Batswana with practical skills which is a requirement when they apply for funding to start projects.

About Us

RAVOS Technical College is a Trust located in Kanye, established in 1986 and registered with the Ministry of Education and Skills Development as a private vocational school in January 1990.
At RAVOS Tech we are a value driven community. Our values ensure that our day to day lives are manageable and make us produce better results.
